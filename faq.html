Getting Started

Companeeds is a premium AI talent matching platform that connects businesses with vetted AI consultants. Think "Toptal for AI" - we personally vet every expert to ensure they deliver real, measurable results for your business.

Our process is simple: 1) You complete our AI-powered intake conversation about your business needs, 2) Our team manually reviews and matches you with the perfect AI consultant from our vetted network, 3) You receive an introduction within 24-48 hours to start your project.

Most companies receive their consultant introduction within 24-48 hours of completing the intake process. Complex or highly specialized requirements may take up to 72 hours to ensure the perfect match.
